Lately

These days some people do breathe but forcefully, they do smile but a fake one, they do enjoy but unwillingly. Everything is so duplicate these days. I don't know if people are lacking knowledge or love or peace but they definitely are in need of something. The drug has become part and parcel of many ones. Does it really sound alright to be a drug addict just because someone broke up with you? Surely not. We have so much to do, so much to offer and so much to get. Suiciding for scoring fewer marks. Are you kidding me? It's so easy to end up your life. But have you thought how hard it was to form a little you? Maybe we all are so lost into ourselves that we forget to see the world. The one with no hands win three medals in swimming championship held in Canada, Vishwas K.S., late Sir Stephen Hawking carried his great works even after being paralysed and many more. Nature is so beautiful and there's so much to explore. People don't really need to look into loses so badly because they have the happiness that can really take over their sadness. Life is so pretty if you have another view towards it. The tree sheds off leaves only to come back to life again, that's how amazing the spring is. The rainbow looks vibrant and it takes rain and sunlight to form it.

After looking into nature and nature cycle, you'll ask me to get practical. But it works in real life as well. You feel alone and has no one to console you, try to do it yourself. You are not incomplete and weak when you are on your own. You are disturbed, you have meditation, you have exercise. You have everything, all you need is your mind by your side. It all starts from within. Always wait, breathe, take time. Never jump into conclusions directly. You wanna cry, do it.You wanna fight and argue, do it. You wanna explain, explain. You wanna ask, ask. Wanna confess, do it. There's nothing impossible and tough. Don't complicate things. Take it easy and go for it. Don't die before your death. Live your life to the fullest.
